ABOUT

PepeNero, nestled in the heart of the city center's old town, is a hidden gem that perfectly blends the charm of historic architecture with modern culinary artistry. Located along cobblestone streets, the restaurant is housed in a beautifully restored building, where the rustic charm of exposed brick and vintage fixtures meets sleek, contemporary design. Inside, the ambiance is warm and inviting, with soft lighting casting a glow on wooden tables and elegant decor that exudes a timeless sophistication.
The menu at PepeNero is a culinary journey that brings together locally sourced ingredients and creative flair. Its offerings are a fusion of traditional and modern European flavors, with dishes that honor the region’s rich history while introducing exciting, contemporary twists. From hearty pastas to expertly crafted meats, every plate at Pepenero is a testament to quality and craftsmanship. The wine list is equally impressive, featuring a selection of both local and international labels, perfect for pairing with your meal.

PepeNero’s cozy atmosphere makes it ideal for any occasion, whether you're enjoying an intimate dinner, celebrating a special event, or simply stopping by for a delightful lunch after a day of exploring the old town. The attentive service ensures that each guest feels like part of the PepeNero family, adding to the restaurant’s reputation for warmth and hospitality. With its combination of delectable cuisine, beautiful setting, and inviting ambiance, PepeNero is a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the heart and soul of the city’s vibrant culinary scene.
